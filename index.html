Our Story

About Regent Stays

Regent Stays was founded on a simple belief: that short-term living should never mean compromising on quality. Each of our properties in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex has been thoughtfully curated to offer the comfort of home with the refinement of a luxury hotel.

From stunning lake views in Las Colinas to convenient DART rail access in Richardson, our collection spans the most desirable neighborhoods. Every suite features designer interiors, resort-style amenities, and the personal touches that transform a stay into an experience.

Whether you are relocating, traveling for work, or seeking a weekend retreat, Regent Stays offers a seamless, elevated living experience. Welcome home.
